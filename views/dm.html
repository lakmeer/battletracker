Battletracker - DM's View

This will become the DM's overview screen. I think it should have two rosters, one which mirrors the roster visible on the Party View, and a secret roster where the DM can park out-of-use or yet-to-be-revealed combatants. Surrouding these lists, an array of buttons and panels allow the DM to create new combatants or do quick calculations, take notes, or send images to the party screen. I'm just spitballing here.

There is no design yet.
